## Summary

Animate the startup `WAVE` logo with a short calm left-to-right reveal.

## Context

MIK-037 added the startup/shutdown splash. MIK-038 added the versioned startup label. The startup card is currently static; the user wants the startup logo itself to have a small animation.

## Scope

- Animate only the startup pixel-art `WAVE` logo.
- Use a short left-to-right reveal across ticks.
- Keep `wave-tui v{CARGO_PKG_VERSION}` and `settling into the signal` text.
- Do not restore the startup `~`, `≈`, or `∿` wave line.
- Keep the blank row below `settling into the signal`.
- Keep shutdown behavior unchanged.
- Keep timing, key-skip behavior, theme usage, playback/search/settings/layout/key mappings unchanged.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Startup logo differs between early ticks and later ticks.
- A later startup tick renders the complete `WAVE` logo.
- Startup still renders no `~`, `≈`, or `∿` wave glyphs.
- Startup still includes `wave-tui v0.1.0` and `settling into the signal`.
- Logo rows remain aligned during reveal.
- Existing shutdown wave animation remains unchanged.
